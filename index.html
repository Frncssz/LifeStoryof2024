My Journey

Quarter 1

Welcoming 2024 by the Sea

As the sun went down, the sky turned pink, blue, and gold. My family and I stood by the shore, feeling the cool wind on our faces. The small waves touched the rocks, making a soft, calming sound. It was the last night of 2023, and we decided to welcome the new year by the sea a place where we always feel at peace. A ferry passed by in the distance, carrying people who, like us, were looking forward to a fresh start. We laughed, talked, and enjoyed the view of the wide ocean in front of us. As midnight got closer, we made our wishes for 2024 hoping for good health, happiness, and success. Standing together, watching the sea, I felt thankful. No matter what happens in the future, I knew that as long as we had each other, every new year would be special.

Quarter 2

A Student’s Journey in the City

Walking through the busy streets, I felt both excited and determined. The tall buildings around me reminded me of how far I had come as a student, chasing my dreams in the city. With my backpack on my shoulder and my cap blocking the soft evening light, I walked toward my next stop whether it was school, a café to study, or just a quiet place to think. The streets were full of life, with cars passing by and people hurrying to their own places. But I moved at my own speed, looking at the big buildings that once felt scary but now felt familiar. I came here to learn, to grow, and to prove to myself that I could reach my goals. As I kept walking, I knew this was only the beginning. There would be hard times, long nights of studying, and moments of doubt. But standing among the tall buildings, I felt a new sense of purpose. One day, I would look back and know that it was all worth it.
